ADMISSION PROCESS

How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to complete your admission process at Forces Academy Faisalabad.

1
Fill the Application Form

Complete the admission form with accurate personal and academic information.

2
Submit Required Documents

Attach all necessary documents for verification and admission processing.

3
Pay Registration Fee

Submit the registration fee through the approved payment method.

4
Receive Admission Confirmation

After successful verification, you will receive your admission confirmation.
